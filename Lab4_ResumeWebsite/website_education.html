about IU Indy

IU Indianapolis is the hub for a new era of innovation—right in the heart of America and driven by students just like you.

As one of the nation’s premier urban research universities, IU Indy is leading the way toward some of the 21st century’s most important advancements in healthcare, artificial intelligence, data science, STEM, and the arts and humanities.

IU Indianapolis launched as an independent campus on July 1, 2024, after a decades-long partnership with Purdue University. For more than 50 years, our campus has differentiated itself as an institution at the forefront of innovation and discovery. We're also a dedicated servant to our community, educating generations of leaders, promoting economic growth, and creating new jobs—in the capital city and throughout Indiana. IU Indianapolis is proudly accredited by the Higher Learning Commission.
